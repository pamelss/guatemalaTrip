Dishes

Pepian

Pepián is a traditional Guatemalan dish of Mayan origin, typical of the department of Chimaltenango. Its origin is pre-Hispanic and it is served in Mayan religious ceremonies. Pepián is an errand that can be prepared with beef ribs, pork, chicken, beef, or a mixture of different meats.

Tacos

Guatemalan tacos are rolled tortillas with a filling in the inside and then fried, like tostadas. Guatemalan tacos are normally served with steamed cabbage, guacamol, tomato salsa, chile sauce, ground dried or fresh Guatemalan cheese, onions and freshly chopped parsley.
